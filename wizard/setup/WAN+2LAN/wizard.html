Use this wizard to set up basic Internet connectivity and local network settings
LAN port (eth0)(Optional) Secondary LAN port (eth0)
Connect eth0 to your local network, for example, a switch that connects to your devices. This address cannot be changed in the wizard. Optionally, connect eth0 to your secondary local network. This address cannot be changed in the wizard.
Internet port (eth1)
Connect eth1 to your Internet connection, for example, the cable modem or DSL modem, and select the connection type.
